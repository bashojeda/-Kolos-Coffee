About Kolos Coffee

We believe that wholeness begins with a rhythm of work and rest. Our mission is to create a space where you can pause, reflect, and enjoy the simple pleasure of exceptional coffee.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Kolos Coffee was born from a passion for creating meaningful connections through exceptional coffee. We source our beans directly from sustainable farms and roast them with care to bring out their unique flavors.

Every cup we serve is a testament to our commitment to quality, community, and the art of coffee making. We believe in taking time to do things right, from bean selection to the final pour.

Our space is designed to be a sanctuary—a place where you can slow down, connect with others, and experience coffee as it was meant to be enjoyed.

Our Values

Quality

We are committed to sourcing the finest beans and crafting each drink with precision and care. Excellence is not just a goal—it's our standard.

Community

Coffee is about connection. We strive to create a welcoming space where relationships flourish and everyone feels at home.

Sustainability

We partner with farms that prioritize environmental stewardship and fair labor practices. The future matters to us.
